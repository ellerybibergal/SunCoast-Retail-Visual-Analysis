SunCoast Retail Visual Analysis:
Overview:
This project explores retail sales and customer behavior using Python and Matplotlib. It focuses on data visualization to understand trends in sales, advertising effectiveness, customer demographics, and product performance across multiple store locations.

Objectives:
Visualize quarterly sales trends over time
Compare performance across store locations and product categories
Analyze the relationship between advertising spend and sales
Explore customer demographics and purchasing behavior
Identify market share distribution by category and location
Build a comprehensive business dashboard

Tools Used:
Python
Pandas
NumPy
Matplotlib

Key Visualizations:
Line charts for quarterly sales trends
Multi-line charts comparing store performance
Grouped and stacked bar charts for category analysis
Scatter plots for advertising vs sales relationships
Histograms and box plots for customer demographics
Pie charts for market share distribution
A full business dashboard combining multiple insights

Key Insights:
Sales peak in Q4 due to seasonal holiday demand
Miami consistently leads in overall sales performance
Electronics is the strongest product category
Advertising spend shows a positive relationship with sales
Customer age distribution varies significantly by location
Mid-range pricing tier generates the highest overall revenue

What I Learned:
How to create different types of charts using Matplotlib
How to visualize time series and categorical data
How to analyze relationships between business variables
How to build dashboards combining multiple visualizations
How to interpret business insights from raw data

Summary:
This project demonstrates how data visualization can reveal meaningful business insights and support decision-making in a retail environment.
